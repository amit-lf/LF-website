Our Perspective

Why contract review is structurally broken

These aren't edge cases. They're built into how contract review works today — and they affect every deal.

Risk Is Structural, Not Surface-Level

Exposure often comes from how clauses interact — not from any single clause read in isolation. Most reviews miss this.

Perspective Changes Everything

The same clause reads differently depending on which side of the table you're on. Analysis must be framed from your side, not neutrally.

You Don't Know What You Don't Know

The riskiest terms are the ones you didn't think to ask about. Systematic coverage matters more than reactive answers.

Industry Context Is Non-Negotiable

Healthcare procurement and semiconductor supply chains have distinct norms and failure modes. Generic review misses them.

Insight Must Drive a Decision

A readable summary isn't enough. You need prioritized actions, renegotiation asks, and a clear verdict — not more text to interpret.
